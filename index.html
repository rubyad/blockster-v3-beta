Tell us about the project.

We’re reviewing applications now. Once the founding Stations are confirmed, V3 launches with the first beta cohort in the coming weeks.

Your Station can syndicate content from across your existing networks. Embed posts, videos, podcasts and community threads from 8 platforms: X, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Reddit, Twitch, Spotify and Apple Podcasts.
After beta: Station plans are expected to start at $149/mo, with Growth plans at $499/mo depending on the services you use. Reward wallet funding is separate: those funds go to your audience as prizes/rewards, not to Blockster. BUX are simple in-platform points users earn for engagement and burn to enter your funded raffles. Rewards funding is self-custodial: your project funds and controls its rewards wallet. We support multiple reward rails including EVM-compatible chains, Solana, and TON, with USDC/USDT/SOL/TON options depending on the rail. Readers get a Blockster wallet when they register, so they can earn and use BUX inside the platform.
