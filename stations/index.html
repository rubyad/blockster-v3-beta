Questions

The details.

What's the catch on "free"?

There isn't one. The $499/mo Hub plan is genuinely free for the first 20 Founding Stations. The only commitment is funding at least $500/mo into your reader rewards — and that money goes entirely to your audience as prizes, never to Blockster.

What is BUX?

BUX is Blockster's onchain loyalty layer for your audience. Readers earn it for reading, watching, and sharing your stories, and use it to win prizes in your station's raffles. You fund the prize pool; BUX is how attention becomes a loyal, repeat, amplifying audience. (Note: BUX is a reader loyalty layer — it's not how contributors are paid. Stations pay contributors directly.)

What if I don't have a content team?

Two answers. First, Electra drafts and publishes for you from a topic, a link, or a forwarded Telegram message. Second, we're recruiting a contributor network — journalists, podcasters, and editors — and Founding Stations get first pick.

Do I have to build everything myself?

No. Activation is white-glove. Many brands already have a hub seeded on Blockster from our community; we hand you ownership and set it up with you. You publish; Electra and our team handle the rest.

When does it go live?

Applications are open now. Accepted Founding Stations are reviewed first and activated with the first beta cohort in the coming weeks.

How exclusive is the founding cohort?

Twenty stations only. We review every application by hand and accept on fit and audience quality — not first-come. Once the cohort is full, new stations join a waitlist at standard pricing.
