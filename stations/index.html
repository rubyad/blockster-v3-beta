What a Station does.

Your Station is the home base for a brand's content and growth loop. Publish once, syndicate across channels, reward readers, and track what actually drives audience growth.

Publish

Stories, video, research

Publish articles, interviews, tutorials, explainers, videos, market updates, product news, and launch content.

Syndicate

Build subscribers who share

Collect subscribers, send email/SMS updates, and reward readers for sharing your stories across their own networks.

Grow

Rewards and referrals

Readers earn BUX points for actions and use them to enter raffles funded by your Station rewards wallet.

Events

Spaces, webinars, AMAs

Publish X Spaces, YouTube webinars, livestreams, AMAs, launches, conference appearances, and IRL meetups.

Electra

AI growth operator

Electra helps suggest content, campaigns, subscriber actions, reward ideas, and daily growth next steps.

Data

Analytics and audience data

Track reads, shares, subscribers, referrals, events, rewards, campaign results, and first-party audience data.
